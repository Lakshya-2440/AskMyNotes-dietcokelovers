# Ask My Notes

### A Verified AI Study System
> Stop trusting hallucinations. Start studying with proof.

---

## The Problem

Students rely on general AI tools:

* Hallucinated answers
* No source verification
* Generic responses

---

## The Solution

Ask My Notes is an AI system that answers strictly from your own notes.

* No external knowledge leakage
* Every answer is grounded in your material
* If the answer is not present, the system refuses to respond

---

## What Makes It Different

### Not Found Guarantee

If the answer is not in your notes, the system explicitly states:
“Not found in your notes for [Subject].”

---

### Subject-Scoped Intelligence

Switching subjects resets the system’s context.
Each subject operates in complete isolation.

---

### Verifiable Answers

Every response includes direct citations from your notes.
No black-box outputs.

---

## Study System

### Knowledge Gap Mapping

Tracks:

* Strong topics
* Weak areas
* Unknown concepts

---

### Practice Engine

* Multiple choice questions
* Short answers with feedback
* Flashcards

Simulates structured oral exams based strictly on your notes.
